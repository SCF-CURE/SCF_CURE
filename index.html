About CURE

The Collaborative Undergraduate Research Experience (CURE) at the State College of Florida is a transformative program designed to integrate authentic scientific research into the undergraduate curriculum. By engaging in hands-on projects, students move beyond traditional textbook learning to tackle real-world problems alongside expert faculty mentors. Our mission is to foster curiosity, develop critical technical skills, and build a diverse community of student-scholars ready for advanced careers in science, technology, and beyond.
